Our Purpose

This trivia game is designed to help users have fun while testing their knowledge across different categories. Developed by Andrew, Judith, Krishnaveni.

Unique Username : Start by entering your unique username to track your scores and compete on the leaderboard.

Select a Category : Pick a category of your choice from the available options, such as Food and Drink, History, or Geography.

Jumbled Questions : Answer a series of jumbled questions within the time limit of 2 minutes.

Score Display : Once the quiz ends, your score will be displayed instantly on the results page.

Leaderboard : Compete with others and secure your spot in the Top 5 Leaderboard. Only the top 5 players with the highest scores are featured!
